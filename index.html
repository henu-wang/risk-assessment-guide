Risk Assessment Guide

A systematic approach to evaluating decision risk

By KeepRule

1

Identify the Risk

List every possible outcome, including unlikely ones. Use pre-mortem analysis to imagine failure scenarios. Explore risk scenarios

2

Assess Probability

Estimate the likelihood of each outcome using base rates, not intuition. Calibrate with reference classes. Learn assessment principles

3

Evaluate Impact

Score the magnitude of each outcome. Consider both financial and non-financial consequences. Watch for asymmetric risks. Run impact scenarios

4

Check Reversibility

Classify decisions as reversible (two-way doors) or irreversible (one-way doors). Apply more rigor to irreversible ones. Decision principles

5

Apply Margin of Safety

Build buffers. If you need X, plan for 1.5X. The margin of safety makes forecasting less critical. Learn from masters

6

Monitor and Update

Track outcomes. Update your risk models with new evidence using Bayesian reasoning. Read our analysis
